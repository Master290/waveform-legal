Privacy Policy

for Waveform

Waveform may collect limited information such as Discord user IDs or server IDs to facilitate bot functionality. We do not collect any personal or sensitive data.

2. Usage of Information

The collected data is used solely for providing the bot's services, such as managing user interactions and tracking bot usage within your Discord server.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data with third parties. The data is only accessible by the bot developers for service-related purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data security, including encryption and access controls. However, no transmission or storage method is entirely secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to fulfill the bot's functionality. If you remove the bot from your server, any associated data will be deleted.

6. User Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of any data collected by Waveform. Please contact us via email for data requests.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Major changes will be communicated through official channels.
